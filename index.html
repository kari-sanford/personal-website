At General Dynamics Mission Systems, I'm exploring the world of IT- where people rely on tech solutions every day. My primary role as a business analyst is to funnel infrastructure demands to the appropriate teams, ensuring end users receive their solutions. I also schedule resources for a domain migration project and an operating system upgrade project. My work on these projects provides employees the environments and tools they need to deliver mission critical solutions.

I am pursuing a Masters of Engineering at Arizona State University in quality, reliability and statistics, with a certificate in Lean Six Sigma Black Belt. My goal is to learn more about Lean Six Sigma methodology, experimental design, and project management, and write those concepts directly to my industry work habits. I will apply Lean Six Sigma to consolidation projects to optimize resources, with the intent of reducing technical debt and maximizing efficiency. I will use experimental design to create new tests for IT architecture, so we can consider more solutions and use cases before making an informed decision. I will execute different styles of project management to engage the diverse teammates on projects that I lead to successful completion. This graduate degree will teach me to thrive as a better engineer from day one.
